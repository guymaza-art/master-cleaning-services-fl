FAQ — HVAC Cleaning in Land O' Lakes, FL

Why do brand-new homes in Epperson and Connerton need duct cleaning?

New construction leaves drywall dust, insulation fibers, and wood particles inside ductwork from the building process. The HVAC system is typically run during construction without filters in place, blowing debris throughout the ducts. Most Land O' Lakes homeowners notice the difference immediately after a first cleaning.

Does the Epperson lagoon community create extra humidity issues for my AC?

Yes — properties near the Crystal Lagoon in Epperson experience elevated ambient humidity, which adds moisture load to your evaporator coil. A dirty coil in a high-humidity environment is a prime condition for mold growth inside the air handler. Annual coil cleaning is especially important in that neighborhood.

Yes — we serve the entire Land O' Lakes area including Connerton, Oakstead, Epperson, Wilderness Lake Preserve, and all surrounding subdivisions. No extra travel charge for any of these communities.

My 2020 home already smells musty from the vents — is that normal?

No — a musty smell from vents in a newer home is a sign the evaporator coil or drain pan has accumulated mold or algae. Florida's humidity makes this common even in 2–3 year old homes. An AC coil cleaning ($99) usually resolves the odor immediately.
