We just bought a home near Lake Hollingsworth in Lakeland — should we clean the ducts first?

Yes, before move-in if possible. Lake-adjacent homes in Lakeland experience elevated humidity from the water, and we frequently find mold growth in duct systems and evaporator coils in homes near the lake. New homeowners should start with a clean system rather than discover HVAC issues after moving in and setting up furniture.

My Lakeland home is in a newly developed area near I-4 — do the new homes need duct cleaning?

New construction always needs a post-build duct cleaning. Active construction sites generate enormous amounts of fine particulate matter — drywall dust, concrete dust, wood chips — that settle into ductwork during the build. This material recirculates for months until professionally extracted. We recommend cleaning within 6–12 months of move-in for all Lakeland new builds.
