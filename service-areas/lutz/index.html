FAQ — HVAC Cleaning in Lutz, FL

Do newer homes in Lutz still need duct cleaning?

Yes — even 2010s and 2020s builds accumulate construction dust, drywall particles, and installation debris in ductwork that was never cleaned after the home was built. Many Lutz homeowners in Long Lake Ranch and Cheval report musty smells and high pollen counts from ducts that have never been serviced.

How often should I clean AC coils in a Lutz home?

Every 1–2 years in the Tampa Bay climate. Lutz sits on the Pasco-Hillsborough border with heavy tree coverage and pollen from surrounding conservation areas. That pollen coats the evaporator coil and restricts airflow, causing the AC to run longer and raise your electric bill.

Do you service gated communities like Cheval and Heritage Harbor?

Yes — we work inside gated communities routinely. Just provide the gate code or let the guard know to expect us. We service Cheval, Heritage Harbor, Long Lake Ranch, and all Lutz subdivisions.

What does dryer vent cleaning cost in Lutz?

How long does a full duct and AC cleaning take in Lutz?
