FAQ — HVAC Cleaning in Brandon, FL

Do you service two-story homes in Brandon?

Yes — that's most of our Brandon work. Two-story builds typically have HVAC equipment in the attic with long supply runs to first-floor rooms. We bring brushes long enough for the runs and pull lint out of long dryer vents that often pack faster than single-story setups.

Are dual-zone systems extra cost?

Yes — each HVAC system gets its own flat rate. A two-system Brandon home with both first-floor and second-floor units priced at $99 + $99. We confirm pricing during the free inspection.

How long does duct cleaning take in a large Brandon home?

A 2,500–3,500 sq ft single-system home: 3–4 hours. Dual-zone setups: 4–5 hours. We schedule the time block based on the walk-through.
