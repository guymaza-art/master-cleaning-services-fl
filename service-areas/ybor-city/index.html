FAQ — HVAC Cleaning in Ybor City, FL

Can you work in Ybor City buildings listed on the National Register of Historic Places?

Yes. We're careful not to disturb historic building fabric. Our technicians work within your existing ductwork without drilling new access points into historic masonry or structural elements unless you specifically request it. We've serviced numerous converted Ybor City buildings without causing any preservation concerns.

I smell a musty odor in my Ybor City loft — is that mold in the ducts?

Quite possibly. Musty odors in Ybor City lofts are frequently traced to mold growing inside ductwork or on evaporator coils, both of which thrive in the humid brick-building environment. We'll do a free inspection to identify the source and recommend the right service — usually our $149 combo covering both ducts and the AC coil.

Do you handle unusual duct configurations in converted commercial buildings?

Yes. Ybor City's converted cigar factories, social clubs, and commercial buildings all have unique duct configurations. We assess each system before starting and use flexible tools that adapt to non-standard layouts. No job is too unusual for us in Ybor City.

How long does cleaning take in a typical Ybor City loft?

A standard Ybor City loft takes 1.5–2.5 hours depending on the duct layout and number of vents. High-ceiling spaces with extended duct runs may take longer. We'll give you an accurate estimate after your free inspection call.
