My kids have asthma — will duct cleaning actually help?

Yes, noticeably so for most families. Ductwork in family homes with children accumulates dust mites, pet dander, mold spores, and other common asthma triggers. Removing these from the air distribution system reduces the daily allergen dose your children breathe. We recommend pairing duct cleaning with a high-efficiency filter upgrade (MERV 11+) for maximum asthma management benefit.
