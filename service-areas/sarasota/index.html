Why Sarasota Homes Need HVAC Cleaning

Gulf-coast conditions create unique air quality challenges that standard mainland cleaning schedules don't account for.

🌊

Salt-Air Corrosion

Homes within a few miles of the Gulf accumulate salt aerosols inside ductwork. Salt attracts moisture, which promotes microbial growth — especially in homes with aging flex duct.

💧

Seasonal Occupancy

Snow-bird homes sitting closed for 4–6 months build up heavy mold and insect debris loads inside ducts. We recommend cleaning before re-opening each season.

🌡️

Year-Round AC Use

Sarasota's AC runs 10–11 months per year. Constant cycling means evaporator coils accumulate dirt faster. A dirty coil can raise your electric bill 15–20% and shorten compressor life.
