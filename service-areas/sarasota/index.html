FAQ — HVAC Cleaning in Sarasota, FL

I have a second home in Sarasota near Siesta Key — how often should I schedule service?

For beach-proximate second homes in Sarasota, we recommend annual service combining duct cleaning and coil cleaning ($149 combo). If the home sits vacant during off-season months, schedule the cleaning just before the main occupancy season so you arrive to fresh air. Salt-air corrosion on idle systems accelerates during vacancy.

Do you service luxury high-rises in the Sarasota downtown and bayfront area?

Yes. We work in all of Sarasota's luxury residential towers including downtown bayfront properties. Our technicians are professional, properly insured, and experienced in upscale condo environments. We coordinate with building concierge and management teams and provide detailed before/after documentation.

My Sarasota home has a whole-house air purifier — do I still need duct cleaning?

Yes. Air purifiers treat the air in circulation but don't remove debris that has already settled inside the ductwork surfaces. Duct cleaning removes the accumulated material from duct walls and returns — material that bypassed or pre-dated the air purifier. Both services complement each other rather than replacing each other.
