Do you service the older concrete block homes in Pinellas Park?

I'm moving into a rental in Pinellas Park — should I request duct cleaning from my landlord?

Absolutely, and you have every right to ask. Most landlords in Pinellas Park don't clean ducts between tenants unless specifically requested. Can you clean AC systems in mobile homes in Pinellas Park?
