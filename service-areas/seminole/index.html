FAQ — HVAC Cleaning in Seminole, FL

Should I schedule cleaning before or after my snowbird season in Seminole?

We recommend cleaning before you leave for the summer and again when you return in fall. Pre-summer cleaning ensures the system is clean going into vacancy. Post-summer cleaning removes mold that developed during the months the home was closed up. If budget is a concern, at minimum schedule a post-summer coil cleaning — that's where the most serious mold growth occurs during vacancy.

Is it safe for seniors with respiratory issues to be home during cleaning?

Yes. Our negative-pressure process contains all debris inside the ductwork and equipment. Nothing is released into the living space during cleaning. We recommend opening a window in an adjacent room during service as a precaution, but there's no health risk for occupants during the process — including seniors with asthma or COPD.

Do you coordinate with property managers for Seminole seasonal rentals?

Yes. Many Seminole property managers schedule us between seasonal tenants. We work with management companies, provide professional invoices for maintenance records, and can accommodate building access through third-party key pickup if needed. Call (813) 285-7449 to discuss scheduling for managed properties.

How do I know if mold is actually in my Seminole home's AC system?

Telltale signs include a musty smell when the AC first turns on, visible dark spots around supply registers, or increased allergy symptoms that appear after turning on the AC.
