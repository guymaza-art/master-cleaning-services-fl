Tampa-Based HVAC Cleaning, Tuned for the Florida Climate

Master Cleaning Services is a family-run HVAC cleaning team that's spent the last decade focused on one thing: getting Florida ductwork and AC systems back to factory-clean condition. Tampa Bay isn't Boston, isn't Atlanta, isn't anywhere else — our climate creates HVAC problems that most generic duct-cleaning playbooks aren't built for.

Average outdoor humidity in Tampa runs 75% or higher. Indoor humidity above 60% turns evaporator coils, drain pans, and duct interiors into ideal habitat for mold, mildew, and dust-mite colonies. Add a 6-month oak pollen season (December through May), salt-air corrosion near the coast, and AC systems that essentially never shut off — and a system that hasn't been cleaned in 4 or 5 years is almost guaranteed to be hauling biological buildup straight into your living rooms.

Every job we run follows the same documented process: a quick HVAC walk-through and access plan, full register protection with drop cloths, negative-pressure extraction with rotary brush agitation on supply and return lines, optional evaporator coil and blower wheel cleaning, EPA-registered sanitizer where appropriate, and a final airflow check at every register. We take before-and-after photos of every duct interior — you see exactly what came out.
